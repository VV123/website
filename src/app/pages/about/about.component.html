About Me

I am interested in transferring artificial intelligence (AI) to real-world applications, particularly in AI for Science. My research focuses on translating complex scientific challenges into formats that AI systems can interpret and process effectively. Specifically, I investigate the following research questions:

Research Questions

1. How can a scientific problem be converted into a format that AI can understand?
  • Can LLMs understand chemical language?
  • How to use AI for more accurate genomic selection?
  • How to use AI for more accurate diagnosis? Will LLMs help?
2. How to enforce human compliance and domain knowledge in open-source LLMs?
  • How to build a private and customized pipeline with open-source LLMs?
  • How to control the security compliance of LLM-generated content (e.g., code)?
